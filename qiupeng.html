| Introduction

Dr. Lin is a Professor in the College of Agriculture at South China Agricultural University. He received his B.S. degree in Life Science (Xiangqin Innovation Class) and M.S. degree in Botany from South China Normal University, supervised by Prof. Hongqing Li. In 2021, he obtained Ph.D. at Institute of Genetics and Developmental Biology,Chinese Academy of Sciences, supervised by Prof. Caixia Gao. In 2023, he completed postdoctoral research with Prof. Caixia Gao, and then started his lab in South China Agricultural University in the same year. Lin studies genome editing technology, especially in developing and optimizing precise CRISPR-based genome editing tools in plants, and applying in agricultural and therapeutic fields. He developed plant prime editors and increased their efficiency via multiple strategies. He also developed a serious of new genome editing technologies for biological research and crop breeding. He has published over 10 first author and co-first author papers in Cell, Nature Biotechnology, Nature Protocols, Molecular Cell, etc. and applied 4 PCT patents.
