QIUPENG LIN, PH.D.

PRINCIPAL INVESTIGATOR

Lin Qiupeng, 32 years old, is a professor and a Ph.D. supervisor, serving as the head of a research group (PI). His research primarily focuses on developing efficient and safe precision editing systems and applying this technology in fields such as agricultural breeding and medical research. He was a pioneer in establishing a guide editing technology system applicable to plants on an international scale. He has also developed multiple new methods to enhance the efficiency of this system. Additionally, he has created a series of novel genome editing strategies and applied them to plant gene function research and molecular breeding. His work in these areas has resulted in several PCT international patents to protect intellectual property in China. Dr. Lin Qiupeng has received support from the "BoXin Plan" and the China Postdoctoral Science Foundation. In recent years, he has published 10 SCI papers as the first author (including co-authorship) in prestigious international journals such as Cell, Nature Biotechnology (four papers), Nature Protocols, and Molecular Cell, with a cumulative impact factor exceeding 350.
